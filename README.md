## 🧩 How to Play

1. Click on either 🪨 Rock, 📄 Paper, or ✂️ Scissors.
2. The computer will automatically pick a move.
3. The result will be shown below with updated scores.

🧾 Rules:
- Rock beats Scissors
- Scissors beats Paper
- Paper beats Rock
